# Making Your App Ready for Use

Once you've configured your app's actions and variables, you can publish the app and control whether it is **private for internal use** or **public on the Ductape Marketplace**.

---

## Step 1: Open the App Overview

1. Navigate to your **Workspace**.
2. Open the **App** you want to configure.
3. Click **Overview** in the app's sidebar.

![App overview screen showing publish button](/img/screens/app-overview-publish.png)

---

## Step 2: Click "Publish"

1. On the **Overview** page, click the **Publish** button.
2. A **Publish App** modal will appear.

![Publish app modal opening from overview page](/img/screens/publish-app-modal.png)

---

## Step 3: Choose App Visibility

In the publish modal, choose how the app should be used.

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| **Private** | The app is available only within your workspace as an internal tool |
| **Public** | The app is published to the Ductape Marketplace for others to discover |

If you choose **Public**, you must also select one or more **Marketplace Categories**.

![Visibility selection showing Public and Private options](/img/screens/app-visibility-selection.png)

---

## Step 4: Upload or Update App Logo (Optional)

You can optionally add or update the **App Logo**.

1. Upload an image file, or  
2. Paste an image URL.

This is especially useful for **public marketplace apps** so users can easily identify your integration.

![Logo upload section inside publish modal](/img/screens/app-logo-upload.png)

---

## Step 5: Publish the App

1. Click **Publish to Marketplace** if the app is public, or  
2. Click **Make Private** if the app is for internal use.

Once completed, the app will become available according to the selected visibility.
