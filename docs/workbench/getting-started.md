# Getting Started with Ductape

This guide walks you through signing up, logging in, and completing the onboarding flow to create your first workspace and product on [cloud.ductape.app](https://cloud.ductape.app).

---

## Step 1: Visit cloud.ductape.app

Open your browser and navigate to **[cloud.ductape.app](https://cloud.ductape.app)**.

You will immediately see a **login modal** with the following options:

- **Email & Password** — log in with your credentials
- **Continue with Google** — sign in via your Google account
- **Continue with GitHub** — sign in via your GitHub account
- **Create an Account** — if you don't have an account yet

![Login modal showing email/password fields and Google and GitHub login buttons](/img/screens/login.png)

---

## Step 2: Log In with Email (OTP Verification)

If you choose to log in with your **email address**:

1. Enter your **email** and **password** and click **Login**.
2. An OTP (one-time password) will be sent to your email address.
3. You will be shown an **OTP entry screen** — enter the 6-digit code from your email.
4. Click **Verify** to complete login.

![OTP entry screen showing 6 digit boxes and a Verify button](/img/screens/otp-validation.png)

> **Tip:** Check your spam/junk folder if the OTP email doesn't arrive within a minute. You can also click **Resend code** on the OTP screen.

---

## Step 3: Create an Account (New Users)

If you don't have an account, click **Create an Account** on the login modal.

1. Fill in the **registration form**:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Full Name** | Your display name |
| **Email Address** | Your work or personal email |
| **Password** | Choose a strong password |
| **Confirm Password** | Repeat your password |

2. Click **Register**, or use **Register with Google** / **Register with GitHub** to skip the form.

![Registration form showing Full Name, Email, Password, and Confirm Password fields with a Register button](/img/screens/create-account-2.png)

---

## Step 4: Confirm Your Email (OTP for Registration)

If you registered with email:

1. Check your inbox for a confirmation email containing a **6-digit OTP**.
2. Enter the OTP on the verification screen.
3. Click **Verify** to confirm ownership of your email address and complete registration.

![OTP verification screen for registration confirmation](/img/screens/otp-validation.png)

---

## Step 5: Welcome — Complete Onboarding

After logging in or registering, you will see the **Ductape onboarding modal**.

This modal guides you through the initial setup. Click **Get Started** to begin.

![Onboarding welcome modal with a Get Started button](/img/screens/onboarding.png)

---

## Step 6: Create Your First Workspace

The first onboarding step asks you to create a **workspace**.

A workspace is the top-level container for your team's products and apps.

1. Enter a **Workspace Name** (e.g., `Acme Corp`).
2. Optionally enter a **Description**.
3. Click **Create Workspace** to proceed.

![Create workspace modal with Workspace Name and Description fields](/img/screens/create-first-workspace.png)

---

## Step 7: Create Your First Product

Next, you'll create your first **product** inside the workspace.

1. Enter a **Product Name** (e.g., `Payment API`).
2. Optionally enter a **Description**.
3. Click **Create Product** to proceed.

![Create product modal with Product Name and Description fields](/img/screens/create-first-product.png)

> **What is a product?** A product is the core building block in Ductape — it is where you connect apps, databases, caches, messaging, and notifications for use via the SDK.

---

## Step 8: You're In! 🎉

Once onboarding is complete, you'll be taken directly to your **product's dashboard**.

You'll see the empty product ready to be configured with apps, databases, storage, and more.

![Product dashboard loaded on screen with empty configuration tabs](/img/screens/product-dashboard.png)
